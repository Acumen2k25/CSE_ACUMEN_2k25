Tournament Format:
•Mode: Squad
•Maps: Bermuda, Purgatory
•Elimination:First round ->Final round
• First Round:
we divide the registered teams into groups(Like Group A and Group B)based on the number of teams registered.The groups will play one Bermuda match.From each group top teams will qualify for next round.(No points calculation just top teams from each will qualify).
• For Final round :
•The qualified teams will play two full maps(Bermuda and purgatory).Top two teams will be decided based on points calculation.
•All the matches will be in esports mode. For example if 20 teams are registered we divide those teams into two groups(10 teams per group).the two groups will play a Bermuda match then the top 6 teams in each group(based on position) will be qualified for final round.Then the commences as given in Final round.
