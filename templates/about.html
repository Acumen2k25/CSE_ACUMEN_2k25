About Acumen 2K25

Acumen is the annual techfest of the CSE department at Vasavi College of Engineering. It brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts for an exciting blend of competitions, workshops, and networking opportunities. With a **cyberpunk theme**, we aim to create a futuristic and immersive experience.
